Akshaya Tritiya Sun 19 Apr 2026 marked the fifth anniversary of the establishment of the Shree Jagannatha Temple in Bath. It remains the only temple in Europe dedicated to Maha Prabu Shree Jagannatha. 

![Grand celebrations inlcuded worship, prayer, storytelling, and excellent refreshments](/assets/images/hindu-5th.jpg)

*The grand celebrations inlcuded worship, prayer, storytelling, and excellent refreshments.*

"It took 21 years of hard work and endurance to establish the Temple in the City of Bath," said Ashish Rajhansha. He asks supporters to enrol as life members.
