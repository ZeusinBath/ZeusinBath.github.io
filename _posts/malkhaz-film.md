Bath Inter Faith Group (IFG) and Bath Preservation Trust collaborated to present a special showing of the film <i>I Will Build This World From Love</i> at the Countes of Huntingford Chapel on 28 March. 

The film describes the moving story of the Peace Project in Tbilisi Georgia,. A Baptist Bishop, The Rt Revd Malkhaz Songulashvili, has constructed a mosque and synagogue to create a Peace Cathedral. Bishop Malkhaz was present to welcome guests and took questions afterwards. 

A Bath IFG group of eight are undertaking a pilgrimage to Tbilisi 8-15 April where we will visit religious sites and celebrate Easter as guests of Bishop Malkhaz.
