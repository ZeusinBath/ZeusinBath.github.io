Bath IFG came together in facilitated conversation Tue 21 Apr in the comfy surroundings of Southdown Methodist Church. Settled into two groups we discussed "what does peace look like to you?" It was a chance to practise respectful listening, and to explore with new friends a deep topic which means a lot to us all. We did not reach hard conclusions; rather it was the process itself that mattered. The time flew by; we could have carried on for longer. We hope to organise further similar events in future.
