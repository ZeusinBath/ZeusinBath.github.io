A Bath IFG party undertook a week's pilgrimage to Tbilisi Georgia in April 2026 to learn how radically inclusive multifaith work is pushing back against a rising tide of division and repression. 

Bath IFG chair William Heath offers a personal perspective of the trip in a [six part write up which starts here](https://wmheath.substack.com/p/bath-inter-faith-group-pilgrimage)

The group will share their experiences in an illustrated talk at Fairfield House, Bath 14 May 1800.
